Turkish Sarma (Stuffed Grape Leaves)

Preperation Time: 40 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 jar grape leaves
  • 2 cups broken rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 200 g minced meat
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Half a bunch of fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried mint
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • a pinch black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. For the filling, start by washing the rice until the water runs clear.
  2. Combine all the ingredients,except for the grape leaves, in a bowl and mix.
  3. If using grape leaves in brine, wash with water to remove excess salt.
  4. Cut off the stems of the grape leaves.
  5. Take one leaf at a time and place the shiny side down.
  6. Put approximately 3/4 tablespoon of the filling near the bottom of the leaf.
  7. Fold the sides of the leaf over the filling, then roll the leaf tightly around the filling.
  8. Once all the stuffed leaves are made, place them tightly in a pot in rows.
  9. Pour water into the pot until all stuffed leaves are covered.
  10. Once the water starts boiling, lower the heat and cook for 45 minutes.
  11. Once cooked, allow them to cool and serve.
